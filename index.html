Scoring Model — Audience Conversion Ratio (ACR)

Built a scoring model to predict concert venue fit using Luminate streaming data. Defined three signals: Catalog Diversity Score (CDS — how spread out listening is across tracks), Hit Dependency Ratio (HDR — share of catalog in Top 20K), and Top Track Strength (TTS — top track dominance). Combined these into an Audience Conversion Ratio (ACR) to estimate each artist's ticket-selling power relative to their streaming volume.
